About Me

Hey everyone! Thanks so much for checking out my website. Let me start off by saying I'm Elaine and I am from Texas. I graduated Summa Cum Laude from DeVry University and have a Bachelors of Science Degree in Multimedia Design and Web Development. I worked as a Web Designer for a financial services company, but now I'm focused on school and gaining more skills to go Front End Web Developer, aka Full Stack Developer.

During my downtime, it's the usual hanging with friends, family, going to the movies/sporting events/concerts. But the hobby that really pushes me to go further in life is punching out some killer beats on my drumset. I discovered back in June of 2018 (after performing at my very first recital) that I have an ear for music. I have been drumming for 10 months, but funny thing is, I am two months ahead in the program. I guess I am a fast learner when it comes to drums.

I love learning new things and I don't want to stop. I am learning how to play the Piano once more, and the Guitar. I have my own band, sing and write the songs.
